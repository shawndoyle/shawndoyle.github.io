Educator

Teaching

Shawn has years of experience teaching both to children and adolescents. He has been a swimming teacher, an educational tour guide, a TA, and lead acting workshops to teenagers. Shawn has spent many summers working at summer camps, and spent three years as the Director of a summer camp. He has worked with children as young as 3 up to post-secondary students. He is currently living in Chongqing, China, where he teaches English to young students.
