Acting and Theatre

Born and raised in Toronto, Canada, Shawn is a passionate actor, creator, and general theatre-person. He holds a Sheridan College diploma in Professional Actor Training and an HBA Specialist Degree in Theatre and Drama Studies from the University of Toronto.

Some of Shawn's credits include George Tesman in Hedda Gabler (Theatre Erindale), Lavrenti Vachnadze in The Caucasian Chalk Circle (Theatre Erindale), Romeo in Romeo Revised (Beck Festival), and Seymour Krelborn in Little Shop of Horrors (Mayfield Theatre). Shawn was a co-creator and cast member of the stage production of How to Make Love in a Canoe, and the Director of plays such as The Proposal (Beck Festival), The Stonewater Rapture (University of Toronto), and The Actor's Nightmare (Mayfield Theatre).
